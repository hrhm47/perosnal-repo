Barn Owl

The barn owl is the most common and is found in most parts of the world. it has seen a huge decline in Ireland and Europe in recent years.

Curlew

The curlew is recognisable by its long curved bill.

Curlews use their long bills to search for worms in mud or very soft ground.

Yellowhammer

The Yellowhammer is a large bunting with a yellow head and a streaked brown back. it usually lives in dry, open coutryside with some tress or hedges.

Lapwing

The lapwing is black and white witha wispy crest.

It is a wader which means you can see it in wetland areas.
